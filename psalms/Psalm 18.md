## Psalm 7:

For all perils and dangers. For long standing patient pronounce this Psalm into water and let him wash with it.

SPECIAL PRAYER FOR THE BSALM

>**ELI-JAH** THE all merciful and mighty God for thine most holy name prevent me from the snares of the enemies and from disease. Take away all evils of the day and night for mighty thy name, my help cometh from thee alone. Hear me by thy mighty of they most holy name **ELI JAH**.
Amen
