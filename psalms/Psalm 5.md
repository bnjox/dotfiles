## Psalm 5:

This Psalm should be used when you require a favor from anyone and pronounce these holy names. **CHANANIAH** into some water and bathe with it do this for 3 days in succession.
 
SPECIAL PRAYER FOR THE BSALM

>By thine most adorable name Chananiah mercifully look upon me and let all people give me their favor especially the fofficial whom I am going to Name Him.
